Our Mission

We believe in value and our name says it all - we sell vacations on a budget. With Budget Vacations, you travel more, spend less.

Our company was founded for those with a desire to travel more on a fixed income. There are many discount websites, and we know it can be overwhelming. We navigate the confusing world of airfare, hotels, and car rentals, so you can be confident you are getting the best prices.

Budget Vacations makes travel simpler and more affordable. We take care of everything, so you can relax and know you are getting the best prices. While the company started with retirees in mind, our vacation packages cater to travelers of all ages on a budget.
